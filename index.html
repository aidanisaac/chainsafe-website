ChainSafe

ChainSafe is the first bio-metrically secured, offline hardware wallet as a service

  • PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

    Extensive research of the Blockchain protocol and tagerted market testing has been conducted during the development of ChainSafe's service - resulting in a simple way for you to interact with digital currencies in the most secure way possible - an offline tablet device which cannot be penetrated through the internet. ChainSafe uses a terminal to act as an offline hardware "cold" wallet which is bundled with our securely configured hardware device; while we offer a Desktop Application for your computer that acts as a "hot" wallet.

  • SERVICE AND SUPPORT

    ChainSafe as a personal Bitcoin/Ethereum platform will be a service which transforms the way we interact with digital currencies - meaning a comforable user experience with a simple, refined interface - and the added security of being insured from any interruption of service. 24/7 technical support will be a hallmark of ChainSafe's commitment to customer service - day or night; we're here to help.

  • ADVERTISING AND PROMOTION

    Advertising and promoting the ChainSafe service is much more than ad placement - it's a holistic approach and philosophy of how we run our business. Privacy by design is an integral part of the ChainSafe philosophy; in an increasingly vulnerable online environment financial security and privacy remains the ciritical objective of our services.

  • STARTING COSTS

    ChainSafe is in the process of researching and devloping our software platform and hardware devices; a massive undertaking which will require the financial capital to exploit our human capital. Our initial round of funding is set at $50,000 - the amount necessary to create a beta prototype of our software. The future of value in a digital world requires new services to securely harness the potential of your investments.
